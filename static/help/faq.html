Tutorials

Step-by-step guides to help you get the most out of Free2Format.

How to Convert a PDF to Word

  1. Go to the Free2Format homepage.
  2. Click the upload box and select your PDF file.
  3. Choose Word (.docx) as the output format.
  4. Click Convert and wait for the process to finish.
  5. Download your converted Word file.

How to Convert Images (JPG, PNG, etc.)

  1. Upload your image file using the upload box.
  2. Select your desired output format (e.g., PNG, JPG, WebP).
  3. Click Convert.
  4. Download the converted image.

How to Convert Audio or Video Files

  1. Upload your audio or video file.
  2. Pick the output format (e.g., MP3, WAV, MP4).
  3. Click Convert and wait for the conversion.
  4. Download your new file.
